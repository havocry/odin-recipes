1 (5 ounce) can tuna, drained ½ cup mayonnaise ¼ cup chopped celery ¼ cup chopped onion 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley ½ teaspoon lemon juice ¼ teaspoon garlic powder ⅛ teaspoon salt ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper 1 pinch paprika, or to taste Directions Combine tuna, mayonnaise, celery, onion, parsley, lemon juice, garlic powder, salt, and pepper in a large bowl; mix well. Season with paprika; refrigerate until chilled.
